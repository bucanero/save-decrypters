# trails-checksum-fixer

A tool to fix the checksum on The Legend of Heroes (PS4) save-games:

- The Legend of Heroes: Beyond The Horizon
- The Legend of Heroes: Trails Through Daybreak
- The Legend of Heroes: Trails Through Daybreak II

### Credits

This tool is based on [crc_fixer_gui.py](samples/crc_fixer_gui.py)
